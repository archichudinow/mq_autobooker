Mapiq desk auto-booker

Books one desk for every weekday in a range. All client-side — nothing is sent anywhere except Mapiq.

How to get your token (paste into DevTools console on app.mapiq.com)

Log in to app.mapiq.com, open DevTools (F12) → Console, paste this, press Enter — it copies your token to the clipboard:

copy((()=>{for(let i=0;i<localStorage.length;i++){let v=localStorage.getItem(localStorage.key(i));try{let o=JSON.parse(v);if(o&&o.credentialType==="AccessToken"&&o.secret)return o.secret;}catch{}}return"NOT FOUND";})())

The token expires in ~1 hour; re-copy if bookings start failing with 401.
